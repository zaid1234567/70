City Montessori School(CMS)

Biggest School In The World

World's Largest School Recognition 2019 was given to CMS by Guinness World Records. "The largest school in terms of pupils is the City Montessori School in Lucknow, India, which had a record enrolment of 55,547 pupils on 16 January 2019 for the 2019-2020 academic year." The school presently has over 56,000 pupils.
